## Overview

Art Text Professional Suite is a complete text-based graphic design platform that serves designers and marketers with the effects depth, template breadth, and export quality needed for professional text design production.

Shape design tools complement text effects with customizable vector shapes, badges, ribbons, and other graphic elements that provide the supporting design components that complete logo and badge compositions.

High-resolution export produces print-ready and screen-optimized files from any composition, with quality settings calibrated for the destination whether that is digital media, large-format print, or embroidery and cutting machine production.

Color and gradient control with full opacity and blending mode support allows fine-tuned color treatment of every element, producing the sophisticated color combinations that professional logo and badge design requires.

Art Text Professional Suite is the text design tool for creatives who need a purpose-built environment for text effects and logo creation, with the depth of materials and effects that makes the results look professionally designed rather than digitally generated.

## Performance & Integration

Art Text is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Art Text processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
